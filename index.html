AboutMe

I love learning psychology, as I think our fundamental understanding of how people think, feel, and behave is critical. I aim to design user-centered products and experiences that solve user and business problems. My process is hands-on, collaborative, and iterative and my goal is to create products and experiences that are meaningful and impactful, and I'm especially interested in utilizing new technologies to improve people's lives in innovative ways.

I attended dozens of classes to study art and photography, which helped me develop visual storytelling and creative thinking. An engineering background provides me with valuable skills and perspectives that can also help me become a successful UX/UI designer.
